Tinolang Manok

Nothing beats this.

Recipe from allrecipes.com

Ingredients
  • Chicken
  • Chicken broth
  • Bok choy
  • Ginger
  • Sayote
  • Garlic
  • Toyo
  • Kangkong
Steps
  1. Sautee the aromatics.
  2. Add the chicken. Cook until brown.
  3. Add the water and broth. Boil for 40 to 45 minutes.
  4. Once the chicken is tender, add the sayote. And other vegetables.
  5. Season to taste.
  6. Serve.
